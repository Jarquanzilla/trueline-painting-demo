About us

Built on doing exterior work right, every time

Trueline Painting Co. started with a simple idea: Cedar Valley property owners deserve a painting contractor that shows up when it says it will, respects the property, and stands behind the work once the crew is gone.

We're based right here at 123 Main St in Cedar Valley. Exterior siding, brick, and masonry are where we spend most of our time — but every job, interior or out, big or small, gets the same level of prep, technique, and finish.

The company is owner-operated — the same person you meet at the estimate is on site making sure the work is done right. That's how you earn a perfect 5-star rating, one job at a time.
